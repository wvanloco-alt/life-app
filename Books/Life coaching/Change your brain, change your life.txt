Limbic or emotional system


         * Sets the emotional tone of the mind
         * Filters external events through internal states ( emotional coloring )
         * Tags events as internally important
         * Stores highly charged emotional memories
         * Modulates motivation and drive, it helps get you going in the morning
         * Controls appetite and sleep cycles
         * Promotes bonding
         * Directly processes the sense of smell
         * Modulates libido


Parts of the limbic system:


Thalamus: Large structure that relays infiormation to and from the outside world
Amygdala: Shaped like an almond and is involved in fear and emotional responses
Hippocampus: Memories into long term storage
Hypothalamus: Emotional center that controls the chemicals that make you feel hungry, sexual, sleepy, exhilirated, angry or unhappy
Olfactory cortex: Sense of smell which connects to emotional and memory centers


When the Limbic system is less active, people tend to be more positive and hopefull. When it is overactive, people tend to be negative and sad


Stimulating the Limbic System:


         * Biological: Supplements, Aromatherapy, physical exercise and nutrition
Supplements: Omega 3 fatty acids that contain higher EPA and S-adenosylmethionine ( SAMe ). For modd changes it is good to have EPA + DHA
Arroma therapy: Lavender oil aroma
Food: Add cinnamon to your cooking ( kaneel )
Exercise: Regular weekly exercise is more effective than any anti-depressant mediciation
         * Psychological: ANT therapy, practicing gratitude and appreciation
Kill your ANT's: Negative thoughts release chemicals that activate the limbic system while positive thoughts do the oposite. Talk back to negative thoughts!
Question your thoughts:
1) Is it true? ( is the negative or stressful thought true? )
2) Can I absolutely know that it is true?
3) How do I react when I believe that thought?
4) Who would I be without the thought? Or, how would I feel if I did not have the thought?
         * Social: Surrounding yourself with people who provide positive bonding, building people skills and having healthy physical contact
         * Spititual: Finding purpose and passion. Doing random acts of kindness
How many people can you show kindness to today?

Heal your gut to boost your brain
The gut is often called the second brain. It is loaded with nervous tissue and is in direct communication with our big brain, which is why we get butterflies when we get excited.
Your gut is one of the most important organs when it comes to brain health.
To be heatlhy, the relationship between good bugs and bad bugs needs to be 85% - 15%. When it starts heading the other way, all sorts of physical and mental problems arise.
Keeping the good and the bad bugs in balance is essential to your mental health. One of the things that kill good bacteria is antibiotics.
The greatest danger from Antibiotics does not come from those prescribed by your doctor, but by those hiding in your food.
